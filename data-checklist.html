Client Data Checklist

This checklist helps prepare a pilot or internal license implementation.

CRM data

  • Account / company name
  • Industry or segment
  • ARR or MRR
  • CSM or account owner
  • Plan / product tier
  • Renewal date or days to renewal
  • Relationship health if available

Support data

  • Zendesk organization mapping
  • Open tickets
  • Critical tickets
  • Ticket age
  • Cancellation/refund/bug tags
  • Chat transcripts if available
  • CSAT or sentiment fields

Billing data

  • Subscription status
  • MRR / ARR
  • Invoice status
  • Failed payments
  • Payment delays
  • Dunning status
  • Cancellation / non-renewal status

Minimum data for a first pilot

Account name, ARR/MRR, renewal timing, CSM owner, support ticket count and billing/payment status.

Best pilot stack

CRM + Zendesk + Stripe/Chargebee gives customer context, support frustration and financial risk in one churn model.
