2–3 Minute Demo Video Script

Use this script to record a short product video with Loom, OBS, Zoom, Canva or CapCut.

0:00–0:20 — Opening

Customer churn rarely happens without warning. The problem is that warning signals are scattered across CRM, support, billing and product usage systems. AI Churn Rescue brings those signals together.

0:20–0:50 — Dashboard

Here we can see total ARR, revenue at risk, priority accounts and savable revenue. The goal is to focus the team on the accounts where action can protect the most revenue.

0:50–1:20 — Executive Insights

Executives get a board-ready summary, weekly action plan, top revenue risks, quick wins, CSM workload and risk by renewal window.

1:20–1:55 — Account Detail

For each risky account, the system explains risk drivers, recommends the next best action and generates a CSM note or customer email.

1:55–2:25 — Connectors

AI Churn Rescue can combine CRM signals from HubSpot or Salesforce, support signals from Zendesk and billing signals from Stripe or Chargebee.

2:25–2:50 — Pilot Offer

We usually start with a 30-day Proof of Value: up to 500 accounts, one data source or connector, executive PDF report and final ROI review. No long-term commitment.
